PT NAWASENA AMERTA EKSPORA

Bringing Indonesian spices to the world market with global quality standards and a spirit of sustainability.

Welcome to PT. Nawasena Amerta Ekspora

PT. Nawasena Amerta Ekspora is a spice export company committed to bringing Indonesia’s natural richness to the global market. We believe that quality, sustainability, and strong partnerships are the key pillars guiding every step we take.

With a primary focus on ginger commodities, we bridge local agricultural produce with global demand — maintaining quality, supporting farmers, and meeting the needs of the international market.

VISION

To become a leading export company that introduces Indonesia’s finest products to global markets, with a strong commitment to quality and sustainability.

MISSION

  • To promote and distribute Indonesia’s top-quality local products to international markets.
  • To maintain high standards of quality, reliability, and punctuality in every export process.
  • To build strategic partnerships with businesses both domestically and globally.
  • To support the growth of local MSMEs (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises) by opening access to global markets.
  • To uphold sustainability, professionalism, and integrity in all of our business practices.

Our Core Value

PT. NAWASENA AMERTA EKSPORA

Quality

We uphold the highest standards of quality at every stage — from selecting the finest ginger, to hygienic packaging processes, and timely international delivery. Our commitment is to provide products that meet global standards and ensure customer satisfaction.

innovation
