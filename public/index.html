A Closer Look at Nawasena

PT. Nawasena Amerta Ekspora is a spice export company committed to bringing Indonesia’s natural richness to the global market. We believe that quality, sustainability, and strong partnerships are the key pillars guiding every step we take.

With a primary focus on ginger commodities, we bridge local agricultural produce with global demand — maintaining quality, supporting farmers, and meeting the needs of the international market.

Our Best Products from Nawasena

Jahe Gajah

Gajah ginger (Zingiber officinale) is a premium Indonesian variety known for its large rhizomes,
low fibre content, and distinctive aroma. It is ideal for use in the food, herbal beverage,
pharmaceutical, and cosmetic industries.

Our Gajah ginger features uniform sizing, long shelf life, and a smooth, low-fibre texture —
perfectly suited to meet the demands of the global market with top export quality.
