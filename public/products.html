Premium Ginger – Zingiber officinale (Jahe Gajah)

Jahe Gajah is one of Indonesia’s finest ginger varieties — known for its large rhizome size, smooth skin, low fiber content, and moderate pungency. Its consistent aroma and long shelf life make it ideal for the food, beverage, herbal, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic industries.

We supply fresh Jahe Gajah that meets global export standards, available in various forms and packaging based on buyer request.

Specification

Skin Color Light yellow, clean, smooth, free of black spots
Flesh Color Bright yellow to yellowish-white
Aroma & Taste Sharp and refreshing aroma, moderately spicy taste
Texture Fibrous-free
Rhizome Diameter 4–8 cm per rhizome
Rhizome Weight 300–500 grams per rhizome
Condition Clean, not rotten, not soft, soil-free
Packaging Options Mesh sack (20–25 kg), wooden box, vacuum pack, or custom export carton

Benefits

Main Health Benefits

  • Rich in gingerol – contains antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties
  • Supports digestion, relieves nausea and cold symptoms
  • Widely used in herbal therapy, health foods, and detox drinks

Our Product Advantages

  • Large, uniform size with excellent visual quality
  • Consistent aroma and flavor – ideal for industrial use
  • Long shelf life – perfect for long-distance export
  • Year-round availability
  • Processed and packed according to international export standards

Premium Cinnamon - Cut Cinnamon Stick

Indonesia is home to some of the world’s finest cinnamon, renowned for its strong sweet aroma and distinctive warm, spicy flavor. With thick bark and a high essential oil content, Indonesian cinnamon is ideal for use in the food & beverage industry, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and aromatherapy.

Our cinnamon is exported in a variety of hygienic, food-safe packaging options to meet international standards. We offer customizable grades, sizes, and packaging to suit buyer specifications and export requirements.

Specification

Skin Color Light brown to dark brown, clean, smooth, mold-free
Aroma & Taste Distinct sweet cinnamon aroma, sweet, spicy, and warm taste
Texture Dry, hard, not soft, not watery
Stick Length 8–45 cm (depending on grade)
Diameter 0.5–3 cm
Condition Clean, insect-free, mold-free, contamination-free
Packaging Options 25–50 kg sacks, plastic inner lining, carton box, vacuum pack (as per export request)

Benefits

Main Health Benefits

  • Contains cinnamaldehyde, known for its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties
  • Helps regulate blood sugar and cholesterol levels
  • Supports immune system and digestive health
  • Acts as a natural aromatherapy for relaxation

Our Product Advantages

  • Rich Aroma & Flavor – Strong sweet aroma and warm, spicy taste unique to Indonesian cinnamon
  • Clean & Mold-Free – Carefully dried and sorted for clean, smooth bark with no mold or contaminants
  • High Essential Oil Content – Ideal for food, beverage, pharmaceutical, and aromatherapy industries
